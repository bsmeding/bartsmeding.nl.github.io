**Network and infrastructure automation** — consultancy and hands-on training for teams that want to move from manual operations to **repeatable, tested automation**, in the data centre, the campus, and the cloud.

If you are looking for **network automation**, **CI/CD for infrastructure**, or **upskilling engineers into automation engineers**, you are in the right place.

## What we deliver

| Area | How we help |
|------|-------------|
| **Consultancy** | Design and roadmaps for **brownfield** and **greenfield** automation: source of truth, inventory, playbooks, pipelines, and safe rollout. |
| **Training** | Tailored workshops around **your** stack and vendors, plus programmes that take **network engineers** toward **NetDevOps** — Git, Ansible, testing, and operations that scale. |
| **Delivery** | **Automated test, deploy, and rollback** patterns so changes are provable, not heroic. Clear **handover** so your team owns what we build. |
