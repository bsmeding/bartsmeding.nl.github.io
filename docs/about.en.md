## b@rtsmeding IT

**Bart Smeding** runs **b@rtsmeding IT B.V.** as a **network and infrastructure automation** practice: **consultancy**, **training**, and **delivery** of automation that survives in production — from **brownfield** onboarding to **greenfield** design, and from **traditional networking** to **tested deploy and rollback** pipelines.

We work at the intersection of **NetDevOps** and **NetAIOps**: solid automation first, then **AI** where it adds clear value (observability, CMDB-backed queries, assisted operations). A longer technical angle is in **[AI in networking](ai/ai_in_networking.md)**.

## Network automation solution architect

I have extensive experience as a **network automation solution architect**: translating **business needs** into **design and implementation**, then **onboarding** both **brownfield** and **greenfield** environments so automation lands safely in real operations.

That work often includes **custom training** built around a customer’s **specific environment** (tools, vendors, and constraints), plus **global training** programs that take **network engineers** toward **NetDevOps** practices—automation, CI/CD, source of truth, and sustainable ways of working.

I help teams **own and extend** what we build: clear **handover**, documentation, and practice so automation does not stop when the project ends.
